# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Walmart's weekly sales performance across multiple stores to uncover business trends, seasonal patterns, and operational insights.

Starting from raw Excel data, the dataset was cleaned and explored using SQL before building an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables business users to monitor sales performance and make data-driven decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze weekly sales performance
- Compare sales across different stores
- Measure holiday vs non-holiday sales
- Identify seasonal sales trends
- Study the impact of external factors like:
  - Fuel Price
  - CPI
  - Temperature
  - Unemployment
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making

# 💡 Key Business Insights

### 🥇 High Performing Stores

Some stores consistently generated significantly higher weekly sales than others, indicating stronger customer demand and operational performance.

---

### 📈 Seasonal Sales Pattern

Sales increased noticeably during festive and holiday periods, showing strong seasonal demand.

---

### 🎄 Holiday Impact

Holiday weeks generally experienced higher sales than regular weeks, making promotional campaigns during these periods highly effective.

---

### 🛍 Sales Fluctuation

Weekly sales varied throughout the year, highlighting the importance of inventory planning and demand forecasting.

---

### ⛽ Fuel Price Impact

Fuel prices showed only a weak relationship with weekly sales, suggesting customer purchasing behavior was not significantly affected.

---

### 🌡 Temperature Analysis

Temperature variations had minimal impact on overall sales performance.

---

### 📉 Economic Indicators

CPI and unemployment rates displayed only a limited influence on weekly sales within the available dataset.
